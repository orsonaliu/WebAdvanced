History

The first FIFA World Cup was held in 1930 in Uruguay. Only 13 teams participated, but the tournament quickly grew into the biggest sporting event in the world.

Over time, the World Cup became more competitive as more nations developed strong football teams. Today, qualifying matches take place across every continent.

Many legendary players became famous through the World Cup, including Pelé, Diego Maradona, Ronaldo, Zinedine Zidane, Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi.

Tournament Format

Teams first compete in a group stage. The best teams move on to the knockout rounds. Each knockout match eliminates one team until only two teams remain for the final.
