Welcome

The FIFA World Cup is the most prestigious football competition in the world. Every four years, countries from around the globe compete for the famous trophy.

The tournament unites billions of people through their shared passion for football. Fans gather in stadiums, homes and public places to support their national teams.

From dramatic penalty shootouts to unforgettable last-minute goals, the World Cup creates memories that last forever.
