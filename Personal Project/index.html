About The World Cup

The FIFA World Cup is an international football competition contested by national teams from around the world. It is held every four years and is the most prestigious football tournament.

Popular Teams

Brazil

Five time World Cup champion and one of the most succsesful teams in the history of the tournament.

Argentina

Known for producing legendary football players and winning multiple titles. They are also the current World CUp champions since they won the tournament back in 2022.

Germany

One of the strongest teams in World Cup history. They are second behind Brazil with four World Cup titles won.

World Cup Facts

  • First FIFA World Cup was held in 1930.
  • It takes place every 4 years.
  • Billions of fans watch the tournament worldwide.
  • Brazil has won the most World Cup titles.
